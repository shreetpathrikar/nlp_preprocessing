    TextBlob

    TextBlob is a Python library designed to simplify natural language processing (NLP) tasks by providing a straightforward and user-friendly API. Developed on top of NLTK and Pattern, TextBlob aims to make it easy for users to perform various text analysis tasks without extensive knowledge of linguistic concepts or complex programming. The library offers a range of NLP functionalities, including part-of-speech tagging, noun phrase extraction, sentiment analysis, classification, translation, and language detection. One notable feature is its built-in sentiment analysis capabilities, providing a sentiment polarity score that indicates the positivity or negativity of a given text. It can also perform advanced sentiment analysis using machine learning classifiers such as the Naive Bayes classifier.
